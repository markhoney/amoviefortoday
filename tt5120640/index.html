Sarbjit (2016)

72%

Biopic of Sarabjit Singh, a farmer residing at Bhikiwind, Punjab, near the Indo-Pak border, crossed the border after having a couple of drinks. However, he was mistaken to be an Indian spy and was sentenced with capital punishment.

Directed by Omung Kumar

Starring Randeep Hooda, Aishwarya Rai Bachchan, Richa Chadha, Darshan Kumaar

Events

  • April 26th, 2012 - Sarabjit Singh is beaten to death by Pakistani prisoners
  • August 28th, 1990 - Sarabjit Singh accidentally crosses the border into Pakistan and is arrested
  • October 3rd, 1991 - Sarabjit Singh is sentenced to death by a Panistani court

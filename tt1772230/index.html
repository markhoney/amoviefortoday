Directed by Li Zhang, Jackie Chan

Starring Jackie Chan, Winston Chao, Bingbing Li, Chun Sun

Events

  • February 12th, 1912 - emperor Puyi abdicates
  • March 10th, 1912 - Yuan Shikai is sworn in
  • April 27th, 1911 - the Second Guangzhou Uprising
  • October 10th, 1911 - the Wuchang Uprising
  • December 29th, 1911 - Sun Yat-sen becomes provisional president of China

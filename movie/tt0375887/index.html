Kamaraj (2004)

50%

The film is a biographical sketch of the former Chief Minister of the then Madras State and the All-India Congress Committee secretary and a respected national figure. The film starts with the young age of Kamaraj and ends with the funeral procession of the leader.

Events

  • April 13th, 1954 K. Kamaraj becomes Chief Minister of Madras Province

  • October 2nd, 1963 K. Kamaraj resigns from his post

  • October 9th, 1963 K. Kamaraj becomes president of the Indian National Congress

Directed by A. Balakrishnan

Starring J. Mahendran, Richard Mathuram, Sampath, K. Vijayan

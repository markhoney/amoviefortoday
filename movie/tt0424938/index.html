A Movie for Today

Rating: 55 %
Length: 99 mins

True story about a woman who falls in love with a violent rapist and murderer. Karla Homolka marries Paul Bernardo despite already having aided him in the rape and murder of her younger sister. Through their marriage he brags to her about other rapes he commits and involves Karla herself in the sexual assault and murder of two other female victims. Told from Karla's point of view.

Events

  • December 23rd , 1990, Karla Homolka and Paul Bernardo assault Karla's sister

  • December 24th , 1990, Karla Homolka and Paul Bernardo kill Karla's sister

  • June 15th , 1991, Leslie Mahaffy, who Tina McCarthy is based on, is kidnapped

  • June 16th , 1991, Tina McCarthy is murdered

  • June 29th , 1991, a fisherman finds Tina McCarthy's body

  • April 16th , 1992, Kristen French, who Kaitlyn Ross is based on, is kidnapped

  • April 19th , 1992, Kaitlyn Ross is murdered

  • May 11th , 2000, Karla Homolka talks with her therapist about her sister

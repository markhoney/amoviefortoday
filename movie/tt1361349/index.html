Drama about the tragic Nanga Parbat expedition by the two Messner brothers in 1970, on which Reinhold Messners younger brother Günther died.

Events

  • June 27th , 1970 Reinhold and Günther Messner reach the summit of Nanga Parbat

  • June 29th , 1970 Günther Messner dies while climbing Nanga Parbat

  • July 3rd , 1970 Reinhold Messner reaches the safety of the Diamir valley

Directed by Joseph Vilsmaier

Starring Florian Stetter, Andreas Tobias, Karl Markovics, Steffen Schroeder

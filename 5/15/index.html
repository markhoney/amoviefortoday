Unstoppable (2010)

May 15th, 2001 a CSX Transportation freight train carrying hazardous chemicals runs out of control

A runaway train carrying a cargo of toxic chemicals puts an engineer and his conductor in a race against time. They're chasing the runaway train in a separate locomotive and need to bring it under control before it derails on a curve and causes a toxic spill that will decimate a town.

The Right Stuff (1983)

May 15th, 1963 Gordon Cooper launches in Atlas 9, ending the Mercury program

Tom Wolfe's book on the history of the U.S. Space program reads like a novel, and the film has that same fictional quality. It covers the breaking of the sound barrier by Chuck Yeager to the Mercury 7 astronauts, showing that no one had a clue how to run a space program or how to select people to be in it. Thrilling, funny, charming and electrifying all at once.

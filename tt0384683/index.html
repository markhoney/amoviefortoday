What We Do Is Secret (2007)

64%

The true-life story of Darby Crash, who became an L.A. punk icon after getting kicked out of high school and forming The Germs with a collection of friends who have little experience with their instruments or playing music.

Directed by Rodger Grossman

Starring Shane West, Rick Gonzalez, Bijou Phillips, Noah Segan

Events

  • April 16th, 1977 - the Germs play their first gig
  • December 3rd, 1980 - the Germs play a farewell gig at Starwood
  • December 7th, 1980 - Darby Crash commits suicide
  • December 23rd, 1979 - the Germs play as "Germs Incognito" at the Whisky a Go Go
